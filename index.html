FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining us in 2026.

Where is DevConf 2026 taking place?+

DevConf 2026 will be held over three days at a central venue, with full address and travel details sent after registration.

What's included in my ticket?+

All tickets include access to keynote talks and the main expo floor. Pro and Enterprise tiers add workshops, VIP networking, and lounge access — see the pricing section above for full details.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?+

Tickets are refundable up to 30 days before the event. After that, we're happy to transfer your ticket to a colleague at no extra cost.

Will sessions be recorded?+

Yes. All keynote and main-track sessions will be recorded and shared with registered attendees within two weeks of the event.

Is there a student or group discount?+

Students get 50% off Standard tickets with a valid .edu email. Groups of 5 or more save 20% — contact us to arrange group registration.
